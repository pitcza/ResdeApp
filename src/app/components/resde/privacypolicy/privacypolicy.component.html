At Re’sIt, we prioritize your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our web application, which is a platform for sharing ideas related to recycling and gardening initiatives to reduce waste in Barangay Gordon Heights.

1. Information We Collect

To provide a personalized experience and enhance community participation, Re’sIt collects the following information from its users:

• First Name and Last Name
• Email Address

This information is required for residents to create an account, post recycling ideas, and interact with others on the platform.

We use the personal information you provide for the following purposes:

We take reasonable precautions to safeguard your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. This includes encryption and secure database management practices.
